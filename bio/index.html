Hi, I'm Stephen. I'm a Full-stack
Developer and STEAM enthusiast.

  I am a Thinkful Full-stack Flex Program graduate. I enjoy a good challenge. My passion is creating interactive webpages that run seamlessly. I have been building apps in JavaScript, Java and Python for about ten years. I have hosted blog sites, the most recent being for my non-profit organization, Mathemagical.org. Another is TechCrowdBacking.com. I also taught Secondary Mathematics in inner-city schools for 5 years. In that time, I’ve had the pleasure of teaching everything from 7th grade mathematics to Second-year Advanced Placement Calculus. I enjoyed my job, and I loved the difference I made in the lives of children.

  However, I made a change to go into web development for many reasons. As the Founder of Mathemagical.org, I tutor children free of charge and am building out the online platform with the hope of one day rivaling Khan Academy. Similarly, TechCrowdBacking.com offers a forum for financial education with the opportunity for users to talk about tech and the companies producing that tech. I am an artist, and the web is my gallery.

  I have been studying all my life. I am passionate about learning. I enjoy self-improvement immensely. In my free-time, I enjoy reading, watching, thinking about Science and Technology, and realistic Science- fiction, watching news, day-trading stocks and general tinkering. Some of my past projects have included hydroponic gardening, building an open- source 3-d printer, building PC’s, and of course, building apps and websites. I enjoy inspiring others to set goals and helping them reach those goals. I love to learn.
